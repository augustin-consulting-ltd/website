Simulating Biological Sample Flows Through Laboratory with Discrete Event Simulation

​

We recently collaborated with Univeristy of Southampton NHS Trust on a simulation project to try to understand and minimise delays of COVID-19 saliva testing.

We worked together to design an discrete event model in python that produces projections for various metrics under different conditions. This enabled us to understand the implications of different triage and cohorting strategies and to quantify the risks.
